Classes for Beginner to Intermediate

Our multi-level classes help players grow from absolute beginner to solid intermediate. Each level is represented by a chess piece, ordered from weakest to strongest:

  • ♙Pawn: Absolute Beginner – Learn basic moves, rules, and learn basic endgames.
  • ♘Knight: Beginner Level – Understand basic tactics, opening principles.
  • ♗Bishop: Lower Intermediate – Learn basic strategical principles.
  • ♖Rook: Intermediate Level – Learn intermediate endgames, intermediate tactics.
  • ♕Queen: Upper Intermediate – Learn more advanced tactics, strategy, and endgames.

1-on-1 Coaching for Advanced Students

My philosophy is that what you put in is what you are going to get out. Students should be practicing daily outside of lessons, and coming to their lessons ready to learn. Openings should be studied outside of lessons (via Chessable or other resources), and students should expect to learn middlegames and/or endgames based on their strengths and weaknesses. My rates are $30 for a 1 hour lesson. However, if money is a problem, I am open to negotiation. I am a full-time student so afternoons and weekends work well for me.
