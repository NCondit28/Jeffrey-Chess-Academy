About Jeffrey ("JP")

Hi! My name is Jeffrey, but most people call me JP. I’m a 15-year-old who’s passionate about chess and helping others improve at the game.

I started playing chess when I was young, and over time I grew to love the strategy, patience, and creativity it takes to win. Whether it’s learning new openings, sharpening endgame tactics, or just having fun with friends, chess has always been a big part of my life.

When I’m not playing chess, you might find me solving puzzles, studying cool strategies, or teaching other players. My goal with Jeffrey’s Chess Academy is to help others discover the same excitement and growth that I’ve found through chess.
